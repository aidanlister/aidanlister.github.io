Virtual Explorer

The Journal of the Virtual Explorer is devoted to rapid electronic publication, particularly in respect to contributions that take full use of the electronic media. We encourage full use of colour, interactive 3D, animation, movies, maps etc.

To take full advantage of the searchable nature of an electronic journal, the entire text content is freely visible. Images and PDF downloads are available only to subscribers, however.

We encourage cross-disciplinary contributions, with an emphasis on ensuring that we publish material from all continents.
